# Venue Notes -- Meridian Theatre, San Pedro

1920s proscenium house, 1,850 seats, recently renovated dock and power service.

## Load-in

- Single dock position in the south alley off 5th Street, dock height, marshal from 05:30.
- Push from dock to stage is level; one freight elevator to the FOH platform level.
- House opens to audience at 18:00; all aisle cable runs must be ramped and dressed by 17:30.
- Truck park and shore power in the alley; generator backup pad at the alley mouth.

## Power

- 400A 3-phase company switch stage left for production; isolated 200A for audio.
- Truck runs on 200A shore service at the dock plus generator backup on auto-transfer.
- House dimmers are on a separate service; no shared neutrals with broadcast power.

## RF Coordination

- All wireless frequencies clear through the production RF coordinator before power-on.
- Venue is 4 miles from the Palos Verdes broadcast cluster; scan on site, do not trust desk plots.
- Axient microphone and FreeSpeak II comms blocks are pre-coordinated; ENG crews must check in.
- Cam 4 RF transmitter operates in the coordinated camera block only.

## Safety

- Hard hats required on deck during rigging calls; high-vis at the dock during truck moves.
- Confetti drop at the finale: lens protection downstage, sweep before audience egress.
- Pyro at item 180 finale is licensed and operated by the venue contractor; 20 ft keep-out.
- Two means of egress from the FOH platform must stay clear; fire marshal walk at 17:00.
- 30 minors on site with the youth orchestra; escorts required in backstage corridors.
