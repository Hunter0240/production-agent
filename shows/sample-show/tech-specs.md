# Technical Specifications -- Harbor Lights Benefit Concert

All plans below are for the 2026-08-22 broadcast from the Meridian Theatre.

## Camera Plan

Six-camera complement, all paths 1080p59.94 to the truck.

| Cam | Position | Body | Lens package |
| --- | -------- | ---- | ------------ |
| 1 | FOH center platform | Sony HDC-3500 | Canon CJ45ex9.7B box lens |
| 2 | FOH house left | Sony HDC-3500 | Canon CJ27ex7.3B |
| 3 | FOH house right | Sony HDC-3500 | Canon CJ27ex7.3B |
| 4 | Handheld, pit and stage left | Sony HDC-3500 | Canon CJ14ex4.3B wide ENG zoom |
| 5 | Jib, house right aisle | Sony HDC-P50 | Canon CJ20ex5B on Stanton Jimmy Jib Triangle, 24 ft arm |
| 6 | Robotic POV, upstage drum riser | Panasonic AW-UE160 | Integrated zoom, presets 1-6 stored at FAX |

Camera notes:

- Cams 1-3 on box-lens sleds with full servo controls and shot boxes.
- Cam 4 carries a wireless RF transmitter pack; spare battery swap at item 150.
- Jib arm sweep clears the FOH mix position by 4 ft; spotter required during audience segments.
- All builds bench-checked before the 13:00 FAX; paint and matching by V1 from the shading bay.

## Audio Plan

- Broadcast mix on a Calrec Brio 36 in the truck, A1 mixing 5.1 with stereo downmix.
- Stage feeds arrive on a 48x16 transformer-isolated split shared with the house mixer.
- RF microphones: 8 channels Shure Axient Digital, frequencies per the venue RF coordination sheet.
- Comms-to-program isolation verified during line check; mix-minus returns for the host IFB.
- Audience reaction: 4 shotgun mics across the lip plus 2 house hangs, time-aligned at soundcheck.

## Comms Plan

- Clear-Com FreeSpeak II wireless beltpacks for camera, stage, and audio floor positions.
- Two-wire PL channels: PL1 production, PL2 camera, PL3 audio, PL4 stage.
- Director calls cameras on PL2; stage manager cues talent on PL4 with show relay.
- IFB 1 feeds the host ear with program-minus; IFB 2 reserved for auction spotters.
- Wired panels at TD, A1, V1, and the production desk; antenna zones cover dock to FOH.
